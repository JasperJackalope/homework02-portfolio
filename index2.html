About Me

I was born and raised in a summer haze, bound by the surprise of our glory days. I didn't mean to turn up out of the blue, univited, but I couldn't stay away, I couldn't hide it. I'd hoped that if you'd seen my face you'd be reminded that for me it isn't over.
